Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Student Life Companion

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Login" button in the navigation menu and select "Sign Up" to create your free account. Fill in your details and you'll be ready to start organizing your student life.

Is the platform free?

Yes! Student Life Companion is completely free for all students. We believe every student deserves access to tools that help them succeed.

Which universities are supported?

Currently, we showcase Ibadat International University as an example. We have plans to expand and support multiple universities across Pakistan in the near future.

Can I access my data from multiple devices?

Once fully functional, you'll be able to access your account from any device with an internet connection, keeping all your tasks and schedules synced.

What features are included?

Our platform includes task management, study planner, campus life updates, and mental wellness tracking - everything a student needs in one place.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Use the contact form above to send us your feedback. We value student input and regularly update the platform based on user suggestions.
